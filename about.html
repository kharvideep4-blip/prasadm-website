The Vision Behind PRASADM

PRASADM (Planning & Resource Automation System for Analytical Data Management) was born from a simple yet profound realization: temples, the spiritual hearts of our communities, needed modern tools to better serve their devotees while preserving their Smart traditions.

Founded in 2020 by a team of technologists and temple administrators, we witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by temple management in handling donations, organizing events, managing inventories, and connecting with devotees in the digital age.

Our mission became clear: create technology that honors tradition while embracing innovation, enabling temples to focus on their primary purpose – nurturing spiritual growth and community service.
