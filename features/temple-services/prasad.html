What is Prasad?

Prasad is the sacred food offered to the deity during worship that is later distributed to devotees as a divine blessing. It symbolizes the grace of the divine and is considered spiritually purifying and beneficial for both body and soul.

Our Prasad Services offer devotees the opportunity to receive blessed food offerings online, book prasadam for special occasions, and have sacred items delivered to their doorstep – connecting you with the divine through the sacred tradition of prasadam.
