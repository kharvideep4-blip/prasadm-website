What is Dawn to Dust Service?

Dawn to Dust Service is a comprehensive temple management solution that covers every aspect of daily temple operations – from the first morning aarti at sunrise to the final evening rituals at dusk. It ensures that all religious ceremonies, poojas, and administrative tasks are performed seamlessly and on time.

Our service is designed to support temple administrators, priests, and staff in delivering a consistent, spiritually enriching experience for devotees throughout the day, while maintaining the sanctity and traditions of the temple.

Daily Schedule

A typical day of temple rituals and ceremonies managed by our Dawn to Dust Service.

5:00 AM – 5:30 AM
Suprabhatam & Morning Aarti Awakening of the deity with sacred chants, flowers, and lamp offering.
5:30 AM – 6:00 AM
Abhishekam & Alankaram Sacred bathing of the deity with milk, curd, honey, and traditional decoration.
6:00 AM – 7:00 AM
Pooja & Archana Morning pooja with mantras, offerings, and archana for devotees.
7:00 AM – 8:00 AM
Prasadam Distribution Distribution of blessed food offerings to devotees.
8:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Individual Pooja Services Booked poojas, abhishekams, and ceremonies for devotees.
12:00 PM – 4:00 PM
Temple Administration Administrative tasks, donation management, and devotees assistance.
4:00 PM – 5:00 PM
Evening Aarti Evening prayer ceremony with lamps, incense, and devotional songs.
5:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Sandhya Aarti Special twilight aarti with elaborate lamps and chanting.
6:00 PM – 7:00 PM
Bhajans & Kirtans Devotional singing sessions and community participation.
7:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Prasadam & Annadanam Evening prasadam distribution and community meal services.
8:00 PM – 8:30 PM
Shej Aarti Night prayer, deity bed preparation, and closing ceremony.
8:30 PM – 10:00 PM
Temple Closing & Operations Temple closing, security checks, and end-of-day reporting.
