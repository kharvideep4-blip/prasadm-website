What is CSR Temple Tourism?

CSR Temple Tourism is a strategic initiative that combines corporate social responsibility with temple heritage conservation, sustainable pilgrimage development, and community welfare. It enables corporations to contribute meaningfully to the preservation of India's rich spiritual and cultural heritage while creating positive social impact.

Through this program, businesses can partner with temples and pilgrimage sites to support infrastructure development, heritage conservation, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment projects.
