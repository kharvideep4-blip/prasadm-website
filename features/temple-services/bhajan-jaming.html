What is Bhajan Jaming?

Bhajan Jaming is a devotional music experience that brings together artists, musicians, and devotees to celebrate spirituality through sacred music. Our sessions feature live bhajan performances, community singing, and interactive workshops that connect hearts through the power of devotion.

Whether you're an experienced musician or a devotee looking to immerse yourself in devotional melodies, Bhajan Jaming offers a welcoming space to explore and experience the joy of spiritual music in a community setting.

Session Types

Different types of bhajan sessions designed for all age groups and musical interests.

🌅
Morning Bhajan Session Start your day with divine melodies – peaceful bhajans and mantras to uplift the spirit.
🌙
Evening Bhajan Sandhya Special evening bhajan sessions with lamps, incense, and soothing devotional music.
🎵
Instrumental Jam Musician-led instrumental sessions featuring harmonium, tabla, dholak, and other traditional instruments.
👶
Children's Bhajan Group Engaging and fun bhajan sessions for children to learn about devotion through music.

Open Mic Bhajan Opportunity for devotees to perform their favorite bhajans and share their talent.
