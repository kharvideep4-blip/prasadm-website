Standard Operating Procedures

Empowering temples with structured protocols for safety, transparency, and spiritual excellence.

A Daily Temple Operations B Priest & Religious Functions C Financial SOPs D Legal & Statutory Compliance E Property & Asset Management F Human Resource SOPs G Infrastructure & Maintenance H Festivals & Events Management I Digital Systems J Community & Outreach
A

Daily Temple Operations

🕊️
Temple Opening & Closing Priest opens sanctum with protocol; logs in register
🪔
Daily Puja Schedule Follow temple‑specific Agama rituals
🧹
Cleanliness & Sanitation Daily sweeping, disinfecting of floors and sanctum
👥
Crowd Management Barricades, queue system, volunteers in uniform
💰
Offerings Collection 2‑person team opens donation box, video‑recorded
B

Priest & Religious Functions

🙏
Priest Appointment Must be trained in Agama/Shastra, background verified
👘
Dress Code Traditional attire; specific for each role
📋
Ritual Inventory Daily checklist for ghee, flowers, dhool, prasadam
🎉
Festival Preparation 15‑30 day prior planning with rituals, logistics, and PR
C

Financial SOPs

💵
Donations (Cash) Daily deposit to bank; maintain counterfoils
📱
Online Donations UPI, website, SMS gateway with auto receipts
📊
Expense Tracking Vouchers with approvals; Tally/ERP entry
📈
Monthly Reports Income‑Expenditure, donation source breakdown
🔍
Year‑End Audit Appoint auditor, prepare Form 10B, ITR‑7 filing
D

Legal & Statutory Compliance

📜
Trust Registration Under Indian Trusts Act or relevant State Act
🆔
PAN, 12A & 80G Registration For tax exemption & donor benefit
🌍
FCRA (if applicable) Separate FCRA bank a/c, quarterly & annual filings
👷
Labour Compliance ESI, PF registration, salary slips, registers
📄
Annual Returns Trust renewal, ITR, FCRA returns (FC‑4)
E

Property & Asset Management

🏗️
Asset Register Maintain log with photos, serial numbers
📝
Rentals Lease agreements vetted by lawyer
🌾
Land Use Monitor encroachments, mutation records
🚗
Temple Vehicles Logbooks, fuel vouchers, servicing records
F

Human Resource SOPs

👤
Hiring Job description, ID verification, service rules
📚
Training Religious rituals, security drills, hygiene
💰
Payroll Salary slips, PF/ESI challans, biometric attendance
🪪
Volunteer Management ID badges, schedules, training manuals
G

Infrastructure & Maintenance

🏗️
Civil Works Annual maintenance calendar, AMC for lifts, A/C
⚡
Electrical Regular inspections, backup systems, safety checks
🔥
Fire Safety Fire extinguishers, alarms, quarterly drills
💧
Water Supply Borewell/tap water testing, storage tanks hygiene
🗑️
Garbage Disposal Tie‑up with municipality or private contractor
H

Festivals & Events Management

📋
Planning Committee meeting 30‑45 days prior
📦
Procurement List items, vendors, budget approval
📢
Publicity Posters, social media, press invites
👥
Execution Volunteers assigned to departments
🚦
Crowd & Traffic Liaison with police, barricading, CCTV live feed
I

Digital Systems

🌐
Website Events, online darshan, donations page
📱
Temple App E‑puja booking, daily calendar, e‑receipts
🖥️
Digital Display Queue status, announcements, aarti schedule
J

Community & Outreach Programs

🍛
Annadanam Menu, donor schedule, hygiene checks
📖
Free Classes Sanskrit, Vedas, Yoga by trained teachers
🏥
Medical Camps Tie‑up with hospitals, basic first‑aid on site

📋 Required Registrations & Certificates

✔ PAN Card (mandatory for banking)
✔ Trust Deed / Society Registration
✔ 12A & 80G (for tax exemption)
✔ FCRA Registration (for foreign funds)
✔ GST (if applicable) for canteen, shops
✔ Shops & Establishment License
✔ Fire Safety Certificate
✔ Building Completion Certificate
✔ Labour Welfare Board Registration (if staff > 10)
